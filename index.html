About 8th Pay Commission

What is 8th Pay Commission?

The 8th Pay Commission is expected to revise the salary structure of government employees in India, with implementation expected in 2026. This calculator helps you estimate your salary under the new pay structure based on latest news and projections.

Latest Fitment Factor

The fitment factor determines how much your current basic pay will be multiplied to arrive at the new basic pay. Based on latest news, expected range is 1.92x to 2.57x, with 2.28x being the most likely scenario.

HRA Calculation

HRA (House Rent Allowance) is calculated as a percentage of basic pay: 24% for metro cities, 16% for tier-2 cities, and 8% for other cities. It's typically calculated as multiples of ₹9,000 as per government norms.

Important Notes

  • This calculator is based on latest news and expected parameters
  • Actual implementation may vary from calculations
  • DA rates are subject to change based on inflation
  • Transport Allowance is fixed based on pay level
  • Grade Pay is optional and may not apply to all employees
  • Always consult official government notifications for final implementation
